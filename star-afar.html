Star Afar

Oh Star afar,
You look so bright.
Is that your light?
I don't believe so
Did you steal?
From weary nights
Or you bore a hole
In sea of time

Curiouser and curiouser
You make my life
Deeper and deeper
You live in my thoughts
I see now,
The kin you hide
Is that it?
The truth of life
Search of madness
Madness of search
Freaky palindromes
And sleeky vibes

Oof, these efforts
Yet nowhere
I feel alone now
I have come far
Alone and afar
Like...like a star
I get it! Efforts!
Efforts, isn't it?
Fuelled the fire
Made you shine
Oh, the pleasure
Of finding things out

Alas! you are tired
From bonds of time
Soon will be gone
The glint of my eyes
But before you go
Please do shine
Farther then ever
Brighter then might
Like I saw you
In my darkest night.
