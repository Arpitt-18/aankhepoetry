A Look At Life

A look at life
With big boggly glasses
Of red and blue frames
And inflated lenses
You may see God!
You may find heaven!
And even a perception
That this is your true vision

A look at life
With now distorted glasses
Of muted red and blue frames
And broken pieces of lenses
You may see demons!
You may find hell!
And a misguided illusion
That life is erosion

A look at life
With no illusive glasses
Your mere eyes and brows
And, of course, eyelashes
You may see humans.
You may find nature.
And truest of all reason
Is your own fascination.
